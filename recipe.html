Explore our famous dishes from around the world.

Handvo

A traditional Gujarati savory cake made from rice and lentils, flavored with spices and baked to perfection.

Dhokla

Dhokla is a savory sponge cake originating from the Indian state of Gujarat. It's a popular vegetarian snack or breakfast item throughout India

Undhiya

The name "Undhiyu" itself is derived from the Gujarati word "undhu," meaning "upside-down." Traditionally, the dish was cooked in earthen pots buried upside down in hot coals. This unique method imparts a distinct smoky flavor.

Dal Dhokli

Dal Dhokli is a beloved dish originating from the vibrant states of Gujarat and Rajasthan in India. It's a wholesome one-pot meal that combines the goodness of lentils (dal) and wheat flour dumplings (dhokli).
